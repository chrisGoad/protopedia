Advantages

Diagrams naturally incorporate prototype structure, and prototype trees provide a uniform method for representing this structure in persistent form . Thus, the technology provides uniform support for the programming task of constructing and saving diagrams. This uniformity is crucial to building an open repository.

In addition, ProtoPedia exposes the prototype realm in its user interface, thus granting new powers to the non-programmer.

Finally, the technology incorporates a general scheme for swapping out elements for others with equivalent functionality. This is useful!
