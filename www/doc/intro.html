Every element of the chart is adjustable with a click. Many are draggable and resizable, for example, the labels "Gold", "Silver", etc.
The Up and Down buttons that appear above the graphics can be used to move up and down the containment hierarchy. For example, after clicking "Gold", if you click "Up", a resizer for the entire chart will appear. Try rezising the chart.
Prototypes Many of the elements of the chart are derived from an adjustable prototype. The right-hand panel displays properties of the selected object, and its prototype if adjustable.
For example, if you click on a bar, you can adjust the fill color of either the individual bar, or its prototype. In the latter case, all of the bars will turn that color (try it).
If you resize one of the labels, it is the prototype that gets resized by default. However, when the adjustment is underway, checkboxes labeled "adjusting this" appear next to each item in the right-hand panel, allowing you to change which element of the prototype chain is being adjusted.
All subparts of the chart, such as the axes, and including the whole chart, are derived from prototype trees. Details on the home page.
