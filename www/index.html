PrototypeJungle
Prototypical inheritance for JavaScript trees
with infographic applications

Development status: proof of concept. Open source, under an MIT license

PrototypeJungle applications are built by successive specialization of tree structures using prototypical inheritance mapped over trees. This technique produces infographs which are UI-adjustable at all levels of specificity, from the general (up the prototype chains) to the specific (down the chains). Adjustment of this kind opens up more space on the spectrum between the algorithmic and the hand-done. You'll see what I mean immediately if you look at an example.
