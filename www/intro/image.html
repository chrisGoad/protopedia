Text and Images

Images (jpgs, pngs, or gifs) and text can be added to most shapes and boxes, and text can be added to connectors as well. To accomplish this, select the item to which you wish to add text or an image. Then the menu in the left panel will enable either or both of the "Add Image" and "Add Text" buttons, according to the operations supported on that item. If you are adding an image, then you will be prompted for its URL. Not every image on the web can be loaded. It depends on the sharing options (CORS) set on the server. For example, every image on WikiPedia/WikiMedia is loadable, but the images on Pinterest are not.

Images and text can also be replaced; the appropriate options will appear when you select an item.

Images without boxes result when you insert an "Image" from the shape tab of the catalog. You are prompted for the URL, and then the image is added without any surrounding box.

As an exercise, add the image of Isaac Newton to the the lower box, and the text "Isaac Newton". https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/39/GodfreyKneller-IsaacNewton-1689.jpg is the URL of an image of Newton.
