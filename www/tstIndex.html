PrototypeJungle
Exposing JavaScript prototype chains for UI-adjustable infographics

PrototypeJungle brings prototype chains out of the opacity of code, and gives new powers to non-programmers. In the initial application area, the structures underlying infographs are visible in a user interface, where all parameters of their construction are available for modification. The enterprise relies on a prototype-oriented component system.

Click on the inspect links to view and edit the prototype structures underlying the infographs.
