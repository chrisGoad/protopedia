An Example: Two Arcs

Here is an example of a PrototypeJungle item: two arcs connected by wavy lines. The example was chosen because it is simple, but at the same time, the variants can be quite interesting to look at. I make no claim of any status as art, but if PrototypeJungle techniques were applied in the context of generative art, the parameters of the art would be editable by anyone, programmer or not (it is of course common for source code to be available with generative art).

What you see at the top is the item in its original form. The succeeding examples involve other parameter settings. Please click on inspect buttons to open the inspector/editor, then explore, click around, and try parameters of your own (some basics of PrototypeJungle usage are explained at the bottom of this page). Click on "?"s for explanations of individual fields. The first thing you might try is setting the line count to 100 or 1000, instead of 10.

You can save what you've done if you sign in. No registration is required: just sign in with your email address or twitter account. (Your email address will not be shared with anyone, and there will be no rummaging about in your twitter world.)
