How to make a diagram.

Inserting.In the initial state of the drawing system , a catalog of visual elements appears at the right of the screen. Elements are inserted by dragging them into the graphics panel. You can select an item by clicking on it. When you select an item, the catalog is replaced by a property-editing panel for the selected item. You can also resize the selected item, and drag it around. You can always bring back the catalog by clicking "Insert" in the top bar.

Replacing.Clicking on "replace" in the top bar puts the catalog in replace mode. In this mode, dragging the replacing element over the element to be replaced, and then dropping, effects the replacement. Different items have different roles (eg "vertex" or "connector"), and only items with the same role as the original can serve as replacement. (See the next section). As you drag over them, replaceable items will be highlighted.

Exercise: Try inserting a few circles, resizing them, and replacing them with other shapes.

"Replace prototype" is just like "Replace", but replaces the prototype of the shape rather than only the shape itself. Prototypes will be explained in a moment.
